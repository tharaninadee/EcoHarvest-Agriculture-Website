About Eco Harvest

Eco Harvest stands as an integral initiative within the Department of Agriculture (DOA) in Sri Lanka.

Eco Harvest is born from a deep-rooted passion for ecological farming and a profound reverence for the Earth. Established by a community of dedicated individuals, we are committed to cultivating a sustainable and greener world.

Join us on our journey to cultivate a better tomorrow. At Eco Harvest, we believe that every seed we sow, every crop we harvest, and every product we offer brings us one step closer to a sustainable, eco-friendly future, a future where healthy farming practices flourish, and the treasures of nature are cherished and preserved.

Together, we can make a profound impact on the world of agriculture and foster a greener, healthier, and more sustainable world for generations to come.

Vision

Our vision is to lead the way toward a future where agriculture and nature exist in harmonious coexistence. We envision a world where each harvest rejuvenates the soil, enriches the ecosystem, and contributes to the well-being of all living beings.

In pursuit of this vision, we are unwavering in our commitment to integrating time-honored agricultural wisdom with innovative, cutting-edge techniques. Our goal is to ensure that every crop we cultivate leaves the land in an even more fertile and abundant state.

Mission

Our mission at Eco Harvest is twofold. Firstly, we strive to transform the landscape of agriculture by championing sustainable and ecological farming practices. We recognize the critical importance of protecting the environment, safeguarding natural resources, and promoting biodiversity in our farming methods.

Secondly, we aim to bring the bounty of eco-friendly farming to your table. We are dedicated to providing you with an array of wholesome, organic products that are not only delicious but also uphold the highest standards of purity and sustainability.
