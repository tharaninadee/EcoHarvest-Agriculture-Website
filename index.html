Welcome to Eco Harvest

Discover sustainable agriculture for a healthier future.

Eco Harvest" is an integral part of the Department of Agriculture (DOA) in Sri Lanka, dedicated to promoting sustainable farming practices.

With Eco Harvest, we are committed to cultivating a more sustainable and environmentally conscious future for Sri Lanka's agriculture sector. Join us in nurturing ecological harmony and elevating your farming experience to new heights.

Our team of dedicated farmers and experts work tirelessly to bring you the finest organic produce, innovative farming solutions, and invaluable knowledge to help you thrive in your agricultural journey.

Our Services

Driven by our commitment to fostering sustainable agriculture, Eco Harvest is more than just a website; it's a vital resource for both farmers and the broader community. Our platform is meticulously designed to empower farmers with knowledge, tools, and services that enhance their farming practices.


By providing comprehensive guidance on eco-friendly techniques, access to expert services, and a wealth of information on sustainable agriculture, we aim to help farmers achieve higher yields while minimizing environmental impact. But our mission extends beyond the farming community – we believe that a thriving agricultural sector means better access to locally sourced, nutritious produce for consumers, ultimately benefiting the health and well-being of all.

At Eco Harvest, we recognize that the impact of sustainable farming extends far beyond the fields. By promoting responsible agricultural practices and supporting local farmers, our website contributes to the overall well-being of communities. When you visit our platform, you're not only gaining insights into sustainable farming; you're supporting a movement that values the health of the planet and the health of its people. Together, we're cultivating a future where farmers prosper, consumers enjoy nutritious, farm-fresh produce, and our shared environment thrives. Join us on this journey towards a more sustainable and prosperous world for all.
